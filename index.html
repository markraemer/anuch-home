Welcome

Anuchaya, otherwise known among her English-speaking friends and colleagues as ‘Anya’, is a DPhil (or PhD elsewhere) student within Applied Linguistics research group at the Department of Education, University of Oxford. Prior to embarking on her PhD journey in the UK, Anya was working as a lecturer in an English Language Program at Mahidol University, Thailand, after having previously completed her Master of Education in Teaching English as a Second Language (TESOL) at Boston University in the USA under the Fulbright scholarship. She is currently working on a doctoral thesis entitled “EFL Teachers’ Cognition of English as a Global Language: Ideologies, Identities, and Practices." Her current research interests include Global Englishes (and related fields), Language Teacher Cognition, and Language Teacher Identity.
