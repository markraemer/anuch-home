2015
Investigating Mobile Health Devices

This project investigated the security and privacy of smart and mobile health applications. I developed a test suite and framework for reverse engineering companion apps and inspecting data flows between devices, apps, and service providers. Results of this project formed my master's thesis at the University of Edinburgh and were published as a poster paper at ACM CCS.

2017
Exploring Home User Privacy

This project explored the use of internet-connected technology in the home. I conducted a series of 36 semi-structured interviews followed by an exploratory survey to understand the social nature of technology use in the home. The project revealed the ways in which technology use in the home is fundamentally communal, organised and arranged for by household members. The papers linked below provide important implications.
